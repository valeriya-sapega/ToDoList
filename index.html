Ready to get started with this to-do list? Here's a quick guide on how to use it:

  • Enter your task in the input field and simply click the Add button to add it to your to-do list. 📝
  • If you've completed a task, cross it out by clicking on it once. ✅
  • Changed your mind about a task? No worries! Just give it a swift double-click to remove it from the list. 🗑️
  • Want to declutter your list by removing all the completed tasks? Click on the Clear Completed button. 🧹
  • Need a fresh start? Click on the Remove All button to clear your entire to-do list in one go. 🚀

Stay organized!
